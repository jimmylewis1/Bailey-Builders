Commercial
Bailey Builders started in 1998 with small tenant improvement projects in malls, office spaces, and strip centers.

After much success, they received requests to travel throughout the western United States. They are licensed in Arizona, Nevada, California, Idaho, Utah, Montana, Oregon, and parts of Wyoming and Colorado.
Bailey Builders have since expanded to include commercial projects and buildings, doing a variety of work for universities, local schools, businesses, churches, and government agencies.
