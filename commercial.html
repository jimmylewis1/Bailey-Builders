Commercial
Bailey Builders started in 1998 with small tenant
improvement projects in malls, office spaces,
and strip centers.

After much success, they received requests to
travel throughout the western United States.
They are licensed in Arizona, Nevada, California,
Idaho, Utah, Montana, Oregon, and parts of Wyoming
and Colorado.
Bailey Builders have since expanded to include
commercial projects and buildings, doing a variety
of work for universities, local schools, businesses,
churches, and government agencies.
Commercial
A family owned and operated business founded in 1998 by Kirk Bailey, Bailey Builders specializes in commercial tenant improvements and building beautiful residential homes.

With over 20 years of construction management experience, Bailey Builders is committed to quality work at an affordable price.
The phrase "commitment to excellence" is not just motivational philosophy for Bailey Builders, it's their mission statement - their guarantee. Driven by the desire to build beautiful, personal homes, Bailey Builders makes every effort to provide their customers with an incredibly individual experience.
